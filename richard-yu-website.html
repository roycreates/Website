The Story

From Biology Student
to Digital Entrepreneur

"I built my first $500K in eight months after graduating — while others were sending out resumes."

Richard Yu graduated Magna Cum Laude from UCLA in 2020 with a degree in biology. Instead of the conventional path, he turned his side hustle — an online consulting business he'd been building since 2018 — into a full-time empire.

Today, Impact Clients has served 3,000+ entrepreneurs and generated over $20 million in combined sales. Richard teaches the exact systems, automations, and AI-powered frameworks he used to build this from a dorm room.
